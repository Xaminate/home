TOP 4 REASONS WHY YOU SHOULD VISIT THE INCA RUINS

 

1. The mountains are BEAUTIFUL

I mean just look at them. They are perfect for hiking, and once you reach the top, you can reward yourself with the view of the ruins.

2. The structures are fascinating and have history behind them

It's not everyday you get to learn about an ancient civilization while standing right there next to it.

3. You can learn to speak their ancient language!

The ancient Inca civilization spoke a language called Quechua. You could learn to speak it? How cool would it be to brag about that you know how to speak an ancient language?
